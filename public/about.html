    • About Gal Kantor

    Dedicated to keeping you active, fit, and injury-free through expert physiotherapy and bespoke injury prevention programs

    At GK Physiotherapy, we believe that staying active and injury-free shouldn't be left to chance. As a chartered physiotherapist practicing in Glasgow City Centre and West End, Gal Kantor has built a practice focused on proactive care, comprehensive injury prevention programs, and personalized treatment strategies that help people of all ages maintain their physical capabilities and quality of life.

    Whether you're an active aging adult looking to maintain independence, a sports enthusiast pushing your limits, or a post-surgery patient working toward full recovery, GK Physiotherapy provides the expert guidance and support you need to achieve your goals safely and effectively.

    Our Philosophy: Prevention First, Treatment When Needed

    The cornerstone of our practice is the belief that preventing injuries is far more effective than treating them after they occur. While we provide expert treatment for existing injuries, our primary focus is on comprehensive injury prevention programs that identify and address potential problems before they become painful, limiting conditions.

    This preventive approach is particularly important for active aging adults who want to decelerate physical ageing and maintain their lifestyle, sports enthusiasts who need to balance training intensity with injury risk, and post-surgery patients who want to prevent future complications. By combining thorough assessments, evidence-based interventions, and ongoing monitoring, we help our patients stay ahead of potential problems.

    When treatment is necessary, we employ a holistic approach that addresses not just the symptoms but the underlying causes of your condition. Our injury prevention programs are designed to ensure that once you've recovered, you stay recovered, with strategies in place to prevent recurrence and protect against future injuries.

    The GK Physiotherapy Approach

    Comprehensive Assessment

    Every patient journey begins with a thorough evaluation of your movement patterns, physical capabilities, injury history, and goals. We take the time to understand not just your current condition, but your lifestyle, activities, and aspirations. This comprehensive approach ensures that our injury prevention programs and treatment plans are truly tailored to your unique needs.

    Evidence-Based Practice

    Our treatment methods and injury prevention programs are grounded in the latest research and clinical evidence. As a chartered physiotherapist, Gal Kantor stays current with emerging techniques and approaches, ensuring that you receive care that reflects the most effective strategies available in modern physiotherapy practice.

    Patient-Centered Care

    You are an active participant in your treatment, not a passive recipient. We believe in educating our patients about their conditions, explaining the reasoning behind our recommendations, and empowering you with the knowledge and tools to take control of your physical health. Your input, preferences, and feedback shape every aspect of your care plan.

    Progressive Programs

    Whether you're working through an injury prevention program or recovering from surgery, your treatment plan evolves with your progress. We regularly reassess your condition, adjust your exercises and activities, and ensure that you're always challenged appropriately without risking setbacks or new injuries.

    Areas of Specialization

    Active Aging and Longevity

    Helping adults aged 50 and above maintain their active lifestyles through targeted injury prevention programs, strength and balance training, and strategies to decelerate physical ageing. We understand the unique challenges that come with age-related physical changes and provide practical solutions that keep you independent and engaged.

    Sports Injury Prevention and Performance

    Working with athletes and recreational sports enthusiasts to optimize performance while minimizing injury risk. Our injury prevention programs address sport-specific demands, correct movement dysfunctions, and build the physical resilience needed for safe participation in your chosen activities.

    Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

    Guiding patients through structured recovery programs after knee, hip, shoulder, and other surgical procedures. We provide the expert support needed to regain full function, prevent complications, and return to your activities with confidence. Our approach combines hands-on treatment with progressive exercise programs tailored to your surgical procedure and recovery timeline.

    Chronic Pain Management

    Helping patients break the cycle of chronic pain through comprehensive assessment, manual therapy techniques, therapeutic exercises, and education about pain mechanisms. Our injury prevention programs often play a crucial role in managing chronic conditions by addressing underlying biomechanical issues and building physical resilience.

    Flexible Care Options: In-Person and Online Consultations

    Understanding that our patients have diverse needs and busy schedules, we offer both in-person and online consultations to ensure you can access expert physiotherapy care in the format that works best for you.

    Our Glasgow City Centre and West End locations provide professional environments for comprehensive assessments, hands-on treatments, and detailed movement analysis. For those who prefer the convenience of remote care, or for follow-up appointments and exercise program reviews, our online consultations bring the same level of expertise directly to your home.

This flexibility ensures that you receive optimal care while fitting physiotherapy seamlessly into your lifestyle.
